Librarian Is My Profession: A History of the People’s Library of Occupy Wall Street

2014

Jaime Taylor and Zachary Loeb

Editor's note: I was a participant in Occupy Wall Street and Occupy Philadelphia, and visited half a dozen other Occupy sites. The People’s Library was one of my favorite places in OWS, and I enjoyed sitting and reading as well as talking with people there.
