Editor's note: A classic. I grew up seeing Toynbee Tiles across the city of Philly, and then in other cities I visited. I tracked them online, read blogs about the tiles, wondered about them, then later met Steve, creator of the documentary Resurrect Dead, and Justin, subject of the film. The collective that wrote up these DIY instructions are influential, creative individuals…
